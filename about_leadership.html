Shehiryar Khan - Managing Partner (ACA)

Shehriyar Khan, a Chartered Accountant (ACA), serves as the Managing Partner at CFAAS, bringing extensive expertise in financial accounting, auditing, and business advisory.

With a deep understanding of UK and international financial reporting standards, he has successfully led complex audits, financial planning engagements, and advisory projects across various industries.

Shehriyar is known for his strategic approach to client relationships, ensuring that businesses receive insightful, tailored financial solutions to drive growth and compliance.

His expertise spans risk assessment, internal controls, and corporate financial structuring, making him a trusted advisor to clients ranging from startups to established enterprises. Under his leadership, CFAAS continues to uphold the highest standards of professionalism, integrity, and innovation in the financial services industry.
