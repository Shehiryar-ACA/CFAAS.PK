Overview

Welcome to CFAAS Accountants, a firm of Chartered Accountants dedicated to delivering exceptional audit fieldwork, accountancy, outsourcing, financial consultancy, and advisory services to businesses and individuals across the globe. Operating from our offices in Islamabad and Peshawar, our experienced team of partners and staff is committed to providing trusted advice tailored to meet your unique needs.

Since our establishment in 2020, CFAAS has built a strong reputation for offering specialist and results-driven solutions to small, medium-sized, and large businesses. With over 20 years of combined expertise gained from leading accounting firms, our partners bring a wealth of knowledge and insight to every engagement. We also hold significant experience in serving large, listed organizations, ensuring top-tier consultancy and accounting services.

At CFAAS, our mission is to thoroughly understand your business and add tangible value to your financial affairs. We strive to build long-lasting relationships founded on trust, mutual respect, and a commitment to excellence.

We take pride in our proactive, forward-thinking approach and personalized service. Our team goes the extra mile to ensure that every client achieves their goals and receives exceptional results. Whether you require meticulous auditing, expert financial advice, or outsourced accounting solutions, CFAAS is here to support your journey toward success.
